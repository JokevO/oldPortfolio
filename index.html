Evolution

Personal

Evolution

Ev0lut!0n is a single player simulation game where you have no direct control on how your city is built. As a player you do not decide what and where to build, but you will be able to assign jobs to the inhabitants. They build their town, but you`ll lead the way. You, as the player, have to make sure that you won’t deplete the resources of the environment to soon, since they take time to grow back. As your world evolves, you will be able to do research in order to increase the productivity or the working and living conditions of your inhabitants. Keep in mind that your knowledge has consequences for your surroundings. As the game continues the village will start to thrive and evolve to the next stadium where you find new challenges on the horizon in other cities, with their problems. This game is made in Visual Studio using the XNA framework. It was designed for entering the Imagine Cup competition in 2011.
